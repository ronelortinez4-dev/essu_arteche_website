Our Story

Eastern Samar State University Arteche Extension Campus (ESSU AEC) has been a pillar of educational excellence in Eastern Samar since its establishment in 2018. We are committed to providing quality education that transforms lives and communities.

Our campus offers a nurturing environment where students can grow academically, personally, and professionally. With dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities, we prepare our students to become future leaders and change-makers.

At ESSU AEC, we believe in holistic education that combines academic rigor with character formation and community engagement.

Our History

Proud legacy of educational excellence

ESSU Arteche Extension Campus was established in 2018 as a community initiative to provide accessible higher education to the youth of Eastern Samar. What began as a small satellite campus has grown into a respected institution known for its academic excellence and community engagement.

Over the years, we have expanded our academic programs, facilities, and community outreach initiatives. Our campus has produced hundreds of graduates who have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields locally, nationally, and internationally.

Today, we continue to build on our rich legacy while embracing innovation and adapting to the changing needs of our students and community.
