Life at ESSU AEC

Campus life at Eastern Samar State University Arteche Extension Campus goes beyond the classroom. We provide students with opportunities to explore their interests, build friendships, and engage in activities that foster personal and professional growth.

Whether through student organizations, sports, cultural events, or community service, life at ESSU AEC is vibrant, inclusive, and enriching.

Campus Facilities

Spaces that support learning and student life

ESSU AEC provides facilities designed to meet both academic and personal needs of students. These include classrooms, computer laboratories, a library, study areas, and spaces for recreation and events.

We continuously invest in upgrading our infrastructure to ensure that students learn and grow in a supportive environment.
